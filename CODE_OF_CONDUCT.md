# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience or viewpoint. We value technical rigor, empathy, clear evidence and respectful disagreement.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, keep critique about the work, acknowledge uncertainty, protect private information and accept correction. Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized attention, doxxing and sustained disruption are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub profile. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, issue warnings, temporarily restrict participation or permanently ban participants according to impact and pattern. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with conflicts of interest delegated when possible.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly. It is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
